I'm a 3rd year Ph.D student at National University of Singapore advised by Professor Li-Shiuan Peh. Before joining NUS, I completed my bachelors and masters degrees from Thiagarajar College of Engineering, India and National University of Singapore respectively.

My research involves (1) building easily integrable next-generation Wearable/mobile systems for real-time, non-invasive monitoring of health biomarkers and (2) developing faster AI-inference enabled body-worn tactile wearable interaction systems. My research interests are Mobile/Wearable Sensing, Human Computer interaction and on-device AI architectures for wearables.
